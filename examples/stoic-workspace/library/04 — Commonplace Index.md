## Search garden

Virtue is trained in particulars. A claim about virtue becomes useful when it names an action. Courage, justice, temperance, and wisdom are not decorations for virtue; they are tests applied to the next choice.

Search for `virtue` with Cmd/Ctrl+F. The matches cross **bold** and *italic* boundaries when the visible word remains contiguous, but they never cross into a separate paragraph or table cell.
