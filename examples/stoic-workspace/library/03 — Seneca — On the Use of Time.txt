# On the Use of Time

> “He tries him, hardens him, and fits him for Himself.”

*Seneca, On Providence, translated by Aubrey Stewart*

Seneca's image is severe: difficulty can be training rather than merely interruption. A modern notebook can retain the challenge without pretending that every hardship is welcome or just.

## Time audit

- What received my best attention today?
- What looked urgent but was merely loud?
- Where did I spend time to avoid discomfort?
- Which difficulty trained patience, candor, or courage?

## A useful distinction

**Busy** is a description of motion. **Useful** is a judgment about direction. They can overlap, but they are not synonyms.
