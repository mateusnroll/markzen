## Editorial policy

- Quotation marks identify source text; unquoted material is commentary or a writing prompt.
- Ellipses are avoided so short excerpts remain legible in context.
- Translation and work are named beside every quotation.
- The generic bust in the illustration is atmospheric and is not presented as an accurate portrait of any philosopher.

The bibliographic companion file `references.bib` is deliberately not a Markdown document, so Markzen shows it as an unavailable tree item.
