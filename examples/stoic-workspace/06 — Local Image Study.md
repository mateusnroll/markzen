# Local Image Study

This page keeps one automatically authorized raster beside its document, making the loaded state easy to inspect without mixing it with failure examples.

![A generic marble bust, journal, olive branch, and hourglass in warm light](assets/stoic-study.png "A self-contained local raster")

Select the image by pointer or keyboard, open **Image Actions**, and inspect its meaningful alternative text and optional title. Canceling leaves the source unchanged.
